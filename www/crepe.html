Crepe

Ingredient

  • 300 g flour
  • 3 whole eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 50 g melted butter
  • 60 cl of milk

Preparation steps

  1. Put the flour in a bowl and form a well
  2. Add the whole eggs, sugar, oil and butter
  3. Mix delicately with a whisk, gradually adding the milk. The dough thus obtained should have a consistency of a slightly thick liquid
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly oil it. Pour in a ladle of dough, distribute it in the pan and wait until it is cooked on one side before turning it over. Cook all the pancakes over low heat.
  5. Enjoy!
