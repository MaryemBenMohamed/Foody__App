Waffle

Ingredient

  • 400 g flour
  • 150 ml milk
  • 150 g pearl sugar
  • 1 sachet of vanilla sugar
  • 200 g of soft butter
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 25 g fresh baker's yeast
  • Un peu de sel

Preparation steps

  1. In a saucepan, warm the milk. Then dilute the yeast in the milk, making sure it is not too hot.
  2. Pour the flour into a bowl. Add the vanilla sugar, salt, whole eggs and finally the mixture of milk and yeast. Knead everything for at least 5 minutes
  3. Cover the dough with a clean cloth and let swell for at least half an hour
  4. Once the time is up, add the soft butter and knead the Belgian waffle dough again by hand (avoid melted butter so that the dough is not sticky.
  5. Incorporate the pearl sugar into the dough. Take the time to mix well. Then divide your dough into 12 small balls
  6. After a quarter of an hour's pause (to rise in volume), the waffle dough is ready to bake
  7. Enjoy!
