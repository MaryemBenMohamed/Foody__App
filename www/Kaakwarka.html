Kaak Warka

Ingredient

  • 1 kg of flour
  • 400 gr of butter
  • 450 gr rose water
  • 500 gr fine almond powder
  • 200 gr of icing sugar

Preparation steps

  1. Preparation of the Dough: Mix the flour with the softened butter (at room temperature).
  2. Add 350 g of rose water and knead until a smooth dough is obtained. Leave to rest for a few hours.
  3. Preparation of the almond filling: Combine the almond powder and the sugar together then incorporate 100 g of rose water in order to obtain a homogeneous texture.
  4. Roll out the dough in a thin layer (1mm thick) using a rolling pin.
  5. Form the stuffing into small sausages (5cm).
  6. Roll up the stuffing with the dough.
  7. Assemble the two ends to form a round washer.
  8. Bake at 180 Â° for 10 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
