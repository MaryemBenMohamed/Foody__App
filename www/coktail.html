Cocktail Juice

Ingredient

  • Apples, preferably red ones
  • Mango
  • Passion fruit
<

Preparation steps

  1. Wash the apples, peel the mango and remove the stone.
  2. Cut the mango and apples into even pieces.
  3. Slice the passion fruit in half, scoop out the flesh and discard the seeds.
  4. Juice all the ingredients.
  5. Pour juice into a glass and add a couple of ice cubes.
  6. Enjoy!
